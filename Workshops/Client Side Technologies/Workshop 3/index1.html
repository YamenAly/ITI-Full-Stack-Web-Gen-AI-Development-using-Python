Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch Pie
30 Min - Prep Time
10 Min - Cook Time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white
  • 2 Tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 3 Tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan...

    • Stir

    • Stir
    • Place Egg
  3. Make Meringue: In a large glass or metal bowl ...
    • Whip egg
    • Add Sugar
    • Spread Meringue
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 Min or until Meringue is goldern brown.
