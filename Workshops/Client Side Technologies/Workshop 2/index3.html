California Backyard Birds
There are many different types of California Backyard Birds. Here are some different birds that you can find sometimes in your very own backyard.
For more information on birds, try going to Project FeederWatch.
I'M A CALIFORNIA TOWHEE GLAMOUR BIRD I'M A HOUSE FINCH BIRD I'M A NORTHERN MOCKINGBIRD
I may be prompted by a tireless knocking at your window or car mirror. I build my nest in cavities, hanging plants, and other cup-shaped outdoor decorations. My mimicking ability, as reflected by my name means 'many-tongued mimic'.
I'M A SONG SPARROW BIRD I'M A WESTERN MEADOWLARK I'M A WESTERN SCRUB JAYBIRD
I use melodious and fairly complex song to delcare ownership of territory and to attract females. My buoyant, flutelike melody can brighten anyone's day. I am a fixture of dry shrublands, oakwoodlands, and conspicuous visitors to backyards.
California Backyard Birds are beautiful creatures!
