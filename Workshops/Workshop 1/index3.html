Create a page named aboutme.html that describes you(you can use dreamweaver or notepad++). On your page, include some or all of the following information:

  • Your Name
  • A description of yourself
  • list of classes
  • your 3 favourite movies
  • 2 images

(you may be tempted to spend all of your 50 minutes on this part of the lab but try to keep the page somewhat short.)
